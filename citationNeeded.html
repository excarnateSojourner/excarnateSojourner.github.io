Citation needed

Users of the English Wikpedia who come across an unsupported claim that should be supported by a citation are encouraged to add the citation needed template after the claim, which renders like this: [citation needed]

What If is a webcomic written by Randall Monroe (who also writes xkcd) in which he gives scientific answers to absurd "what if...?" questions. He has made a running gag of adding "[citation needed]" after claims he makes that are painfully obvious (example).
