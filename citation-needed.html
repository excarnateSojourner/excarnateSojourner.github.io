Citation needed

Users of the English Wikpedia who come across an unsupported claim that should be supported by a citation are encouraged to add the citation needed template after the claim, which looks like this: [citation needed]

What If is a webcomic written by Randall Monroe (who also writes xkcd) in which he gives scientific answers to absurd "what if...?" questions. He has made a running gag in What If of adding "[citation needed]" after he makes a statement that is obviously true (example).
